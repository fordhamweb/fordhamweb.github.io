That year, the capital hosts a grand festival.
Your stepmother sews new clothes for Cám so she can attend — but she refuses to let you go. Instead, she sets a cruel task: “Here are two baskets of rice mixed with beans. Pick out the rice from the beans, and the beans from the rice. When you finish, you may go to the festival.”
As soon as she and Cám leave, you look at the huge piles.
You could never finish this alone.
