From your ashes, a fruit-bearing tree grows beside a quiet road.
Its golden fruit releases a sweet scent that drifts far across the fields.
One day, an old woman passes by and begs the tree:
“Golden fruit, drop into my basket.
I promise to never eat you.”
The tree responds. A single luminous fruit falls gently into her hands.
She takes it home.

Clean her home and cook her meals
when she is away to repay her
