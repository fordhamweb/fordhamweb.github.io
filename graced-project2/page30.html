The old woman worked long days at her market stall, gone from morning to night — yet each evening she returned to a tidy home and a warm meal.
One day she pretended to leave and saw you step out of the fragrant fruit.
Overjoyed, she broke it open and adopted you.
Later, the King passed through the distant market.
At her stall, he tasted a piece of betel arranged in the style he recognized as yours.
Shaken, he asked who prepared it.
When you stepped forward, he knew instantly — and the two of you joyfully returned to the palace together.
