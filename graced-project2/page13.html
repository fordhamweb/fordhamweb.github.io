His voice is warm and steady:
“Your stepmother has killed the fish,” he tells you kindly.
“But do not despair. Its spirit can still stay with you.”
He explains what you must do:
“Find the bones.
Place them into four jars.
Bury each jar beneath one leg of your bed.
In that way, the fish will remain with you — quietly watching over you.”

Wipe your tears and nod.
You will find the bones.
