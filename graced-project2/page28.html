Unfortunately for Cám, you are far from gone.

You threaten her for taking your husband.
Cám's face drains of color.
She can no longer stay in the same room as the loom, let alone weave on it.
Terrified, she drags the loom far from the palace grounds.
There, trembling, she sets it on fire.
When only ashes remain, she scatters them to the wind and hurries back to the palace, convinced that this time, she has finally destroyed you.
She is wrong — again.
