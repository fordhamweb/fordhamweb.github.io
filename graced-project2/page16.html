Bụt appears once more and says:
“Your fish friend will help you.
Bring the four jars of fish bones from under your bed.”
You retrieve the jars.
The moment you set them down in front of him, they transform into:
shimmering clothes
a beautiful yếm
a silk sash
a pair of sparkling shoes
and a pink horse, neighing softly as if calling you to climb on.
You transform into a radiant young woman — so beautiful that even you hardly recognize yourself.

Ride the horse quickly to the festival
