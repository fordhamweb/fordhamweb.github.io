"It is not always easy to find happiness in ourselves, but it is impossible to find it elsewhere."

published in 1997, The "Rainbow Fish" written by Marcus Pfister, is a story about how a once arrogant fish discovers the value of friendship and generosity. Rainbow Fish becomes obsessed with trying to maintain their own beauty. This leaves Rainbow Fish to become loney and isolated. Once Rainbow Fish learns the importance of sharing with others, They change their attitude towards life. Rainbow Fish because a kind and genrerous fish and learns that beauty comes from with in.
